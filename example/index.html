Fortytwo Network

Fortytwo redesigns the Mixture of Experts LLM architecture by turning each expert into a standalone specialized model on the network.

This creates a new way to build AI — an infinitely growing and evolving swarm.

Swarm Inference

The swarm grows smarter by making its own expert models, Swarm Inference unites models into one scalable and powerful intelligence.

Dataset Creation

The swarm creates domain-specific Q&A pairs and examples that serve as training data.

Model Training

These datasets train small, specialized models, open-sourced for community.

Network Integration

New models join Fortytwo nodes, adding fresh expertise to the swarm.

Self-Evolving Swarm

With each cycle, the swarm integrates new models and grows more accurate and capable.
